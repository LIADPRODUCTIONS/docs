##  Ways to login

You can either login by creating an account with your email and password, or choose the discord login option for simplified access.

### 1. Create your account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Using Email">
    1. Head to https://deploy.acenodes.co.uk
    2. Select the yellow **Register** Link below the login form

       ![image.png](/images/image.png)
    3. Enter in your desierd username

       ![Screenshot2025 05 30031612 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-30031612.png)

       <Note>
         Username must be at least 3 characters long and contain only lowercase letters, numbers, periods and underscores.
       </Note>
    4. Enter in your email address

       ![Screenshot2025 05 300316122 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-300316122.png)
    5. Enter in your password, and enter it again to confirm.

       ![Screenshot2025 05 3003161224 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-3003161224.png)

       <Note>
         Passwords must be at least 8 characters long. Passwords must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one digit, and one special character.
       </Note>
    6. Verify your email

       <Info>
         An email will be sent to the address provided with a link to verify your account.

         If you get any issues while trying to verify your account, please reach out to our support team.
       </Info>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Using Discord Login">
    1. Head to https://deploy.acenodes.co.uk/
    2. Select **Login with Discord**

       ![343453435 Pn](/images/343453435.png)
    3. Log in to your discord account

       ![image.png](/images/image.png)
    4. Select Authorise

       ![75923756823786832 Pn](/images/75923756823786832.png)

       Thats it, You are now logged in to AceNodes using your discord account.

       Your AceNodes account email will be the same one that you used to register for your discord account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Alt accounts are strictly prohibited, If our systems detects an alt account; It will automatically ban all accounts from your IP.

  You may appeal an alting ban through our discord server.
</Warning>
